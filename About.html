ICHIKAMI BRAND

Ichikami is a hair care product company focused on bringing out our client's best appearance with our products. In the field of toiletries & cosmetics, we offer daily necessities like body washes, shampoos, hair conditioners, styling products, bath salts, depilatory products, basic skin care products, as well as products for business/professional use including those for beauty salons, hotels, golf resorts, and urban spas.

BRAND PHILOSOPHY

Perfection through the finest, purest and most effective ingredients for your hair. Simplicity without extra additives or untested ingredients. Confidence in every hair-friendly product.

NOTHING MORE, NOTHING LESS

A longer ingredient list does not translate to more benefits. Our “Nothing more, Nothing less” philosophy ensures your hair gets the ultimate care it deserves. Through rigorous research and the strictest selection criteria, We are committed to retaining only the most beneficial haircare ingredients for maximum effectiveness.

PERFECT & SIMPLE

As part of our commitment to producing perfect, simple and remarkably safe products, we never use unnecessary additives, colorants, fragrances or mineral oil that would only burden the skin. Embrace the beauty of simplicity with us, and see how the right care will help your skin blossom.
