  • Ichikami has been studying Japanese hair from the essence for many years.
  • Humidity, temperature, water hardness, the common problems of hair among people.
  • Trendy hair care methods.

  • We have come up with a shampoo that makes us happy today.

  • We have incorporated the blessings of Japan's beautiful four seasons.
